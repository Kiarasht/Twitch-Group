Twitch Grouper

Twitch Grouper is a simple android application that significantly improves the user experience of twitch users as a member and as a streamer. The application provides tools for both category of users allowing them to get the maximum experience from Twitch. Twitch Grouper uses Twitch API to perform actions such as look at today's hot games and streams for a user and starting a commercial on your video if you are a partnered twitch streamer.
